About Africa's Pot

At Africa's Pot, we bring the authentic flavors of West Africa to the heart of Lagos. Our chefs use time-honored recipes passed down through generations, combined with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that transport you straight to the villages and bustling markets of Nige
